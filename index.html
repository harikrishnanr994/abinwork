The Reverie of Music

Our music band is launching on 17th August 2018


Kerala is well known for its traditional art forms and music. Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, the two classical dances of India that developed and remain popular in Kerala, have added vibrant colours to the rich cultural heritage of the state. Bharathanatyam also is popular here. And these dances grab the attention of the global audience in a big way. To experience this great heritage, VJ presents 'Nirvana', a concoction of nritta, nritya and natya. You will be mesmerized by Malayalam melodies and fusion songs along With their dance-translations.

Time is the greatest game changer. It challenges everything, even the greatest works of art. But, some creations can withstand the test of time. They are from the masters who sculpt on time. And their masterpieces survive that greatest test. In music too, there are evergreen pieces of art. Born in the past, still fresh at the present and will remain fresh in the future too. We call them evergreen songs. They were created not just for the generation to which they belong, but for the happiness of all the mankind of all the time. VJ has a special show comprising evergreen songs, which has the hits of 70's, 80's 90's etc.

There is nothing in the world so much like prayer as music is. It points to the divine rationality and beauty of God. 'Geethanjali', the devotional songs show by VJ, offers its listerners a unique mystical and spiritual experience. Our dedicated and experienced musicians have a slew of evergreen devotional songs to fill your hearts with heavenly bliss. Blessed are our artists with the magical flute of Krishna, the resonating Damaru of Shiva, the soothing veena nadam of Saraswathi Devi... Here music pray for us; the prayer that can rejuvenate every soul.

Old is not only gold, if it is about the Malayalam film songs of the golden Seventies. For Keralites, those songs are more precious than gold. The period upto late 1970's is considered the golden period of Malayalam film music. Melodious songs adorned with the beauty of lyrics and soul-filling music were the highlights of those era. VJ presents the music show 'Memories' for those who have a heart resonate with the nostalgic tunes of those grand old days. No doubt, our gifted singers help you to travel back in time, to that colourful world of Black & White 'Ormakal'.

Kerala, God's own country, has a great tradition of folk music. To showcase and popularise this unique music heritage, VJ has curated a folk music show nameed Kathirmanikal. It is a rare collection of folk songs from various parts of Kerala. The show is presented by a set of talented singers and performers whose renderings are marked by the fragrance of the soil.

Comedy shows are one of the most loved stage performances in Kerala. In earlier days it was just imitating film stars and mimicking some sounds. Later, it grew into a crowd pulling form of contemporary art and mega comedy shows came into being. VJ presnets a Comody Show too. It has a good team of comedians and mimic artsits including film and television stars.

Music has only one language. Still, it has many voices and they come from many worlds. East and West- both have great traditions in music. In India, South has a huge treasure of Carnatic music and the North has Hindustani. The fusion wizards of VJ have the expertise in mixing the best of all the worlds, various genres and styles. Yes, 'Fusion' proves that music has only one language, just like love. Crossing all the boundaries and barriers, music flows like a never ending river and so sings our Fusion Band.
